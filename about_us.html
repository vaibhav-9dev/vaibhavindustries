Our Heritage

history_edu A Legacy of Heat & Flavor

Deeply rooted in the fertile lands of Guntur, Andhra Pradesh, our journey began decades ago. The region's unique black soil and scorching sun create the perfect environment for the world-renowned Guntur Sannam chillies.

We bring the authentic, fiery essence of India's most famous chillies from the farm directly to your kitchen, preserving the bold color and pungent heat that Guntur is legendary for.

Sourcing from Guntur Farmers

Direct Farm Relationships

We work directly with over 500 small-scale farmers in the Guntur region, ensuring they receive fair prices and sustainable support for their traditional farming methods.

Traditional Processing

Our chillies are sun-dried and cold-ground to preserve the essential oils and volatile compounds that give Guntur chilli its distinctive aroma and kick.
