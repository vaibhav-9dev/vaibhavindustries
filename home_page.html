Our Legacy: Rooted in the Soil of Guntur

For over three generations, we have been part of Guntur's world-renowned chilli spice trade. Our process begins in the fertile black soil of the Krishna river basin, where local farmers cultivate the most pungent varieties of chillies.

We don't just sell spice; we sell a tradition. Each batch is carefully inspected by elders who can tell the quality by the aroma alone. By choosing Guntur Heritage, you are supporting sustainable farming and authentic Indian culinary heritage.
